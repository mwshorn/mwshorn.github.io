Horn Simulator

Follow these instructions to simulate the valve movement on a French horn:

1. Place your left index finger on the keyboard's letter 'E'.

2. Place your two corresponding fingers on 'W' and 'Q'.

3. Your fingers are now in position! Press down these keys with those fingers to press down the valves.
